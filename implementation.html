Overview

A phased rollout aligned to the Social-Ecological Model, enabling quick wins and sustainable change.

Phases & Milestones

Phase 1 — Discovery (Weeks 1–4)

  • Stakeholder mapping (parents, schools, employers, council, NHS partners).
  • Baseline survey: activity levels, barriers, preferences.
  • Venue scouting & safeguarding checks.
  • Risk assessment & data protection plan (draft).

Phase 2 — Design (Weeks 5–8)

  • Co-design 30–45 min family sessions + micro-workouts.
  • Private digital community space/LMS.
  • Employer incentives (flex-time, wellness credit).
  • Marketing assets (posters, social, newsletter copy).

Phase 3 — Pilot (Weeks 9–12)

  • 3–4 pilot events (parks / community halls).
  • Collect feedback (attendance, NPS, brief wellbeing scale).
  • Refine timetable, childcare, comms cadence.

Phase 4 — Rollout (Months 4–6)

  • Monthly calendar & sign-up flow.
  • Home workout library (video + printable routines).
  • Employer partnerships & recognition scheme.
  • Participation & engagement dashboards.

Phase 5 — Sustain & Scale (Ongoing)

  • Train parent champions & volunteers.
  • Quarterly KPI reviews; iterate content & schedule.
  • Explore additional funding & sponsorships.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Programme Lead: delivery, partnerships, risk.
  • Instructors: session delivery, family adaptations.
  • Comms Lead: marketing, social, parent updates.
  • Data/Eval Lead: consent, surveys, KPI tracking.
  • Parent Champions: peer support, local activation.

Risks & Mitigations

  • Low attendance: pre-registration, reminders, incentives, partner hosts.
  • Weather/venue: indoor alternatives, clear cancellation policy.
  • Safeguarding: DBS checks, code of conduct, child-first design.
  • Data privacy: minimisation, opt-in consent, secure storage.
