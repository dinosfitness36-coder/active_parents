Methods

  • Quantitative: Brief PA surveys, attendance, optional wearables.
  • Qualitative: Focus groups/interviews; thematic analysis.
  • Process: Fidelity checklists, barriers log, adaptation tracker.

Ethics, Consent & Data Protection

  • Informed, opt-in consent; right to withdraw.
  • Data minimisation, secure storage, anonymised reporting.
  • Safeguarding and inclusion (accessibility, language, culture).

Reporting

  • Monthly: Participation dashboard.
  • Quarterly: Outcomes vs targets; recommendations.
  • End-of-cycle: Impact summary and case studies.
