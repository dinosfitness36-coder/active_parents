Design & Approach

  • Design: Pre–post evaluation with comparison group (if feasible).
  • Population: Parents/guardians participating in the programme (record age, gender, postcode, baseline PA).
  • Timepoints: T0 (baseline), T1 (end of pilot), T2 (3–6 months follow-up).
  • Consent & GDPR: Written consent, privacy notice, data retention schedule, anonymisation procedures.

Data Collection & Analysis

  • Instruments: Short surveys (5–10 min); optional accelerometer/subsample devices.
  • Qualitative: 4–6 parent interviews or 1–2 focus groups on barriers, facilitators, and feasibility.
  • Analysis: Descriptives; paired tests (pre–post); thematic analysis for qualitative data.
  • Equity: Monitor differences by demographics and area-level deprivation.
