üìö Theoretical Framework

The Social-Ecological Model (Bronfenbrenner, 1979) explains how behaviour is shaped by multiple layers of influence: individual, interpersonal, organisational, community, and policy. It is widely used in sport development to design inclusive, multi-level interventions.

By applying this model, the Active Parents Project ensures that solutions are not only practical but also sociologically informed and strategically aligned with UK policy structures.
